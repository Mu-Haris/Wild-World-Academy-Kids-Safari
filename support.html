Q: What age group is this app designed for?

A: Wild World Academy Kids Safari is designed for children ages 3-8. The trivia questions, animations, and voice-overs are all crafted to be fun and educational for young learners.

Q: Does the app require an internet connection?

A: An internet connection is needed to display advertisements. However, the core educational content and gameplay work offline.

A: The app features multiple wildlife zones including Savanna, Ocean, Rainforest, Arctic, Farm, and Backyard. Each zone contains unique animals with fun trivia questions and facts.

Q: Is my child's data collected?

A: No. We do not collect any personal information. All game progress is stored locally on your device. Please see our Privacy Policy for details.

Q: The app is crashing or not loading properly. What should I do?

A: Try these steps:

  1. Make sure your device is running iOS 17 or later
  2. Close the app completely and reopen it
  3. Restart your device
  4. Check for app updates in the App Store
  5. If the issue persists, email us with your device model and iOS version
